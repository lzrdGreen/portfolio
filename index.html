Recent Developments

In 2024, compact LLMs emerged as a promising area of research.

I'm currently exploring the Qwen2.5-0.5B-Instruct model, a compact LLM with 0.5 billion parameters, in my spare time, focusing on its potential for resource-constrained environments. My comparative assessment examines various fine-tuning approaches (SFT and DPO, both independently and in combination) against each other and the baseline model. Evaluation is based on perplexity (PPL), a metric that quantifies how well the model predicts the next word or token in a generated text.
